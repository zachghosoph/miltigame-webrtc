Line-Up!

Home Rules

Rules

Objective

Be the first player to reach 10 points. You can line up your checkers in any direction, as long as they are connected.

How to Set Up

  1. Choose how many players there will be or if there will be a computer opponent.
  2. Flip the in-game coin to decide who goes first.

How to Play

  1. On your turn drop one of your colored checker into one of the columns.
  2. If you added onto an existing line of checkers, roll the in-game die. If you roll any value less than the length of the line you added onto, gain a point. If not, no points are added and the game continues.
  3. Lines can only be rolled for if they have been added onto on this turn.
  4. The next player's turn begins.

How to Win

Be the first player to reach 10 points or, in the event of a tie, whoever has the most points wins.
